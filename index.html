About Me

Hello! I'm a full stack web developer who enjoys solving problems and creating things that add value to the world. I have experience turning ideas into responsive, accessible frontend design with a RESTful API backend.

I graduated from Colorado State University with a BS in Mathematics where I also ran on the track and cross country teams. After college I built predictive models as an analyst at a data marketing company, and created a relational database and maintained a website for a higher ed nonprofit.

These experiences provided me with a strong foundation to continue my coding journey at Lambda School, a full stack web development and computer science program. In this program I learned new skills daily and worked on teams to build apps meeting project specifications within a week span. Now, I'm ready to apply these skills in the industry!
